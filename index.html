Our Mission

A Hub For All Traders, Worldwide.

At TRADEZZ, we aim to be a hub for traders from all walks of life around the world, to come together to discuss their ideas, share their calls and charts for second opinions, and connect with others who share the same interests.

We understand the importance of community support when it comes to trading, and we believe our community can provide the necessary support and camaraderie to help you grow as a trader.

Community Standards

Server Rules

Our rules exist to protect every member and uphold the quality of our community.

01

Respect Everyone

Treat all members with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks will be tolerated.

02

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Avoid spamming messages, links, or promoting personal services without permission.

03

No False Claims or "Guru" Behavior

Do not present yourself as a financial expert or guarantee profits. Be honest and realistic.

04

Quality Over Quantity

Keep conversations meaningful and relevant to trading, learning, and growth.

05

Help and Support Each Other

Experienced members guide beginners. Beginners feel free to ask questions.

06

No Financial Advice

All discussions are for educational purposes only. Always do your own research before any decision.

07

Respect Privacy

Do not share personal information — yours or others' — without explicit consent.

08

Encourage Active Participation

Engage in discussions, share insights, and contribute to the community's collective growth.

09

Use Channels Appropriately

Keep discussions in relevant channels to maintain organization and clarity.

10

Follow Admin Instructions

Decisions made by admins and mods are final and must be respected to maintain order.

Let's keep this server a focused, respectful, and growth-oriented space for all traders.
— infinix_ (spandy)

Full Curriculum

The Trading Roadmap

Foundational
Market Structure

Identify swing highs, swing lows, breaks of structure (BOS), and changes of character (CHoCH) to read what price is truly doing.

→
Foundational
Trends

Understand bullish and bearish trends, how to classify market phases, and how to trade in alignment with the dominant direction.

→
Foundational
Trend Shifts

Learn to spot early reversal signals — when a trend is losing momentum, and how to position ahead of a confirmed directional change.

→
Intermediate
Supply and Demand

Map institutional supply and demand zones, understand how price reacts to these areas, and how to use them for precise entries and exits.

→
Intermediate
Liquidity & Inducements

Understand where stop-loss clusters form, how smart money hunts retail liquidity, and how to avoid being the one they target.

→
Intermediate
Liquidity & Daily Cycles

Learn how the market moves in predictable time-based cycles — Asian, London, and New York sessions — and where liquidity is formed and taken each day.

→
Advanced
FVG — Fair Value Gaps

Identify imbalances in price delivery, understand why price returns to fill gaps, and use FVGs as high-probability entry models.

→
Advanced
ERL & IRL

Master External and Internal Range Liquidity — the draw-on-liquidity concept that reveals where price is engineered to move next.

→
Advanced
IFVG — Inverse Fair Value Gaps

Learn how FVGs that are violated flip into resistance or support — and how to use Inverse FVGs as refined re-entry points in trending markets.

→
Advanced
Premium & Discount

Understand the equilibrium of any range — buy in discount, sell in premium. The golden rule that governs smart money positioning.

→
Essential
Risk Management

The foundation of longevity in trading. Master position sizing, risk-to-reward, stop placement, and how to protect your capital above all else.

→
Mindset
Psychology

Conquer the mental game — discipline over impulse, patience over FOMO, and the emotional consistency that separates professionals from gamblers.

→
Mastery
Journaling & Refinement

Document every trade, review every mistake, and build a feedback loop that continuously sharpens your edge and compounds your growth.

→
